## Highlights

- **Born rule, selected internally** *(theorem)* — Within the ribbon's steering
  class, no-signaling forces the Born exponent p=2 uniquely. The import of
  partially entangled ensembles is closed: steering is generated internally by a
  shifted source measure, robust under five measure deformations. `968aaa5`, `c152c98`
- **Mechanical steering premise, form-free** *(measured)* — An annealed,
  field-magnetized, fast-frozen elastic source supplies a polar-biased measure
  carrying steering diversity in the 3rd and 5th moments (h=0 control null);
  no-signaling selects p=2 on ensembles produced entirely by the elastic
  dynamics. The measure fits no closed form — and none is needed. `2080bbe`, `1c653b6`
- **Class-M precedence theorem** *(theorem)* — For the class of all
  outcome-definite pair-resolution mechanisms: CHSH S>2 is achievable **iff** the
  mechanism admits a shared, statistically invisible precedence structure
  (preferred foliation, a per-run shared coin, or a single joint event). The
  frameless coin reaches S=2√2 without signaling and leaves its order invisible. `c8e1bf3`
- **The amplitude seam** *(reduction)* — Reaching the quantum value from a local
  mechanism is barred by Bell's theorem; three registered attempts fail on a
  trilemma of horns (no steering / no amplitude / superluminal telegraph). The
  seam is crossed only by joint, nonlocal resolution. `324f097`, `2053106`
- **The 1/3-cosine point** *(landmark)* — The isotropic-cosine ceiling
  E=cosθ/3, S=2√2/3, reached independently three ways (relaxational dynamics,
  the lever rule, frame-local resolution). `a043f8f`, `49e8c1b`
